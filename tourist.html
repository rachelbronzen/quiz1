Tourist Places in Surabaya

This is my favorite places in Surabaya that I would recommend you to go there.

JALAN TUNJUNGAN
iconic street, filled with shops and a vibrant atmosphere.

PAKUWON MALL
luxurious shopping mall interconnected with other malls

PASAR ATOM
traditional shopping center offering variety of local goods and foods with a truly authentic and vibrant market atmosphere.

KENJERAN PARK
recreational park featuring playgrounds, gazebos, viewing decks, and souvenir shops.
